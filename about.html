About Caroline

Nutritionist, Holistic Wellness Coach, and someone who has walked this road herself.

My story

I know what it feels like to be stuck.

For years I battled weight cycles, exhaustion, and health concerns that nobody seemed able to solve completely. As a wife, mother, and working professional, I felt pulled in every direction — and my own health was always the last priority.

It wasn't until I started paying close attention to what I was eating — and connecting those choices to how I felt — that everything began to shift.

"When I cut out processed foods, prioritized whole nutrition, and made real space for self-care, the transformation was dramatic — and it wasn't just physical. My energy came back. My clarity came back. I came back."

That personal breakthrough ignited a passion I couldn't ignore. In 2012, I founded Serenity of Body and Mind to walk this journey with others — as a knowledgeable guide who truly understands what it feels like to struggle, and what it feels like to finally thrive.

My approach

No two people are alike. Neither are their paths to wellness. My programs are built entirely around you — your body, your lifestyle, your goals — not a one-size-fits-all template.

Whole-person care Food, emotions, movement, and mindset — we address all of it together.
Truly personalized Your plan is built around you, not a generic protocol.
Sustainable, not extreme Real changes that last — not crash diets or quick fixes.
Joyful & flexible Your health journey should feel good, not punishing.
