My Story

I know what it feels like
to be stuck.

For years I battled weight cycles, exhaustion, and health concerns that nobody seemed able to fully solve. As a wife, mother, and working professional, I felt pulled in every direction — and my own health was always the last priority.

It wasn't until I started paying close attention to what I was eating — and connecting those choices to how I felt — that everything began to shift.

"When I cut out processed foods, prioritized whole nutrition, and made real space for self-care, the transformation was dramatic — and it wasn't just physical. My energy came back. My clarity came back. I came back."

That personal breakthrough ignited a passion I couldn't ignore. In 2012, I founded Serenity of Body and Mind to walk this journey with others — as a knowledgeable guide who truly understands what it feels like to struggle, and what it feels like to finally thrive. My practice is fully virtual, which means I can work with clients across the country from the comfort of their own homes.
