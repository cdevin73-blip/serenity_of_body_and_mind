Every Serenity coaching program includes the app — a digital companion that keeps you accountable, connected, and supported between sessions.

📓

Daily Journal

Set your daily intention, log meals by time of day (morning, afternoon, evening), track water intake, write your gratitude list, record medications, and reflect on your day — everything your printed journal had, and more.

For clients
💧

Habit Tracking

Track sleep, water, exercise, nutrition, and mood every day with simple controls. Add custom habits specific to your program. Watch your streaks grow day by day.

For clients
✨

AI Coaching Insights

The app analyzes your week's habits and generates personalized observations — celebrating wins, spotting patterns, and offering specific, encouraging guidance.

Messaging with Caroline
