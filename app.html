Everything in one place

Designed to replace your printed journal
and so much more

Every Serenity coaching program used to include a printed journal. The app replaces it completely — and goes far beyond what any paper journal can do.

📓

Daily Journal

Set your daily intention, log meals by time of day, track water intake, write your gratitude list, record medications, and reflect on your day — exactly like the printed journal, but always with you.

For clients
💧

Habit Tracking

Track sleep, water, exercise, nutrition, and mood every day with simple, satisfying controls. Add custom habits specific to your program. Watch your streaks grow.

For clients
✨

AI Coaching Insights

Powered by AI, the app analyzes your week's habits and generates personalized observations — celebrating wins, spotting patterns, and offering gentle, specific encouragement.

For everyone
💬

Direct Messaging

Message Caroline directly inside the app. No more searching for her email or phone number — your coaching relationship lives in one place, always accessible.

For everyone
📊

Progress History

See your 7-day and 30-day habit history at a glance. Watch your patterns emerge over time and celebrate how far you've come since day one.

For clients
🌿

Coach Dashboard

Caroline can view your progress, read your journal entries (with your permission), leave notes, adjust your goals, manage reminder settings, and monitor your streaks — all from her own view.

For Caroline
🔔

Smart Reminders

Morning and evening nudges to log your habits and fill in your journal. Caroline can customize your reminder schedule based on your lifestyle and when you're most likely to engage.

For everyone
🎯

Personalized Goals

Your daily targets aren't generic — Caroline sets and adjusts them specifically for you as your program progresses. Your sleep target, water goals, and exercise minutes evolve with you.

Set by Caroline
📋

Weekly Curriculum

Caroline can push weekly resources, educational content, and program materials directly to your app — recipes, guides, reading material, and more, right when you need them.
